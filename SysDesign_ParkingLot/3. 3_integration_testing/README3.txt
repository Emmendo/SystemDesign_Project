Integration Testing:
Definition - Tests that aggregate results from various parts and sources.

Full Ticket Purchase (Card Payment): Allows the customer user to click to purchase a ticket, pay by card, allowing for card processing, and the customer can choose to have a receipt printed with the ticket.

Full Ticket Purchase (Cash Payment): Allows the customer user to click to purchase a ticket, pay by cash, allowing the customer to pay the exact amount or receive change back, and the customer can choose to have a receipt printed with the ticket.

Pay at Gate: Allows the customer to choose the option of getting a ticket with the timestamp on it and paying later, which bypasses paying with card or cash for now.
